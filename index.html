Recovery & Regeneration

Growth happens outside the gym. Master recovery to maximize your gains.

Sleep Optimization

7-9 hours of quality sleep is non-negotiable. Dark room, cool temperature, consistent schedule. Your muscles rebuild while you rest.

Nutrition Timing

Post-workout protein within 2 hours. Adequate carbs for glycogen replenishment. Hydration throughout the day, not just during training.

üßò

Active Recovery

Light movement on rest days. Yoga, walking, swimming. Keep blood flowing without taxing your system. Mobility work prevents injury.
